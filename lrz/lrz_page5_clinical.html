Behavioral Analysis

Cross-referenced explanations

Behavioral input is matched against evidence-based clinical patterns — sundowning, pain signaling, environmental sensitivity — surfacing the most probable causes.

Persona Modeling

Individual-level learning

Unlike generic tools, Lrz_limited builds a longitudinal profile of each patient. Corrections you make train the model — making it more precise with every interaction.

Handoff Support

Transfer-ready patient context

Auto-generated behavioral summaries that support shift handoffs — so incoming staff aren't starting from zero on known patients.

Decision Support

Intervention suggestions, not mandates

Lrz_limited surfaces options — you decide. Designed to augment clinical judgment, not override it.
